Harry Sanders shared this I've never been more confident about demand for SEO in 2026 Everyone's saying AI is going to kill agencies completely, AI is about to create the biggest wave of SEO demand we've ever seen We're already seeing it in our pipeline, it hasn't slowed down since last year, and people jumping in to learn SEO in Hawk Academy has been faster than it was 5 years ago when we first launched it Most businesses don't realise the mess AI is creating behind the scenes, here's what I'm seeing: 1. AI website builders don't consider SEO fundamentals, they all build with client-side javascript builds out of the box, fast websites to deploy, but Google can't see half the content at times, the more of these that go out, the more there will be to fix, that's half the job in SEO ready for us to fix up 2. AI needs constant steering, I've been using Claude to fix up Hawk Academy dev issues and it goes on the weirdest tangents, you'll ask it to fix a 404 and it'll say done, you check a week later and it's not only not done, it's changed three other things you never asked it to touch, multiply that across a big brand with thousands of pages and you've got a mess that only a human with SEO experience can untangle 3. Businesses that are going hard on AI content, publishing hundreds of blogs, they may see an impression spike, that quick dopamine hit, but as soon as a human reads that page and bounces off, you're not going to see traction long term, long term you'll need to focus on unique perspectives, information gain, and expertise by real humans to stand out in SERPs Every one of these problems needs a human with actual SEO experience to fix And the funny part is Anthropic, OpenAI, they're all had job ads up for SEO roles this year If the companies building AI need SEO specialists, what makes you think your business doesn't? public_profile__posts
