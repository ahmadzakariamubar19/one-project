Cyrus S. shared this Listen, I don't want to put down anybody's LLMs.txt files, but I found this pretty insightful... Our friend Przemysław Charchan watched server logs for 900 domains over 6 months He observed over *45 million requests* from AI bots Here's the kicker: Out of 45 million requests, not a single AI bot requested an LLMs.txt file "Over more than half a year – let me remind you, across ~900 domains – I recorded only 1227 requests for these files (on average about 6 requests per day). This traffic concerned 107 domains.... Among the requesters there was not a single real AI bot." (Now you could argue that Dataprovider—one of the aggregators that actually crawled the LLMs.txt files—is selling this data to all the AI companies, in which case we'd expect to see far more requests, but I suspect that's a long shot) Well researched and written.
