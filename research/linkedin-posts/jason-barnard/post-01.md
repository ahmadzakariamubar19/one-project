Jason Barnard shared this Most entrepreneurs I speak to are already using AI assistants. They’ve built them. Set them up. Started using them. And still say the same thing: “It works… but not consistently.” The issue is rarely setup. It’s how the AI is being used. A few principles matter more than everything else: Put your most important instructions first and last. Be explicit with context, don’t assume the AI knows your situation. Don’t ask it if it agrees with you. Ask for objections and improvements instead. If conversations drift, restart them with a clean summary. That’s usually where performance changes. Better inputs.
