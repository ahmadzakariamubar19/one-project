Kevin Indig shared this ChatGPT judges a page by its cover Pages with headlines that directly answer the question get cited 41% of the time. Pages with loosely related headlines drop to 29%. I partnered with AirOps on a study of 815,000 query and page pairs across 16,851 queries and 10 industries.
